🌍 Why Regional Pandits Matter

India has hundreds of regional pooja traditions that differ by state, community and even village. A Bengali Durga Pooja requires specific rituals like Sandhi Pooja that only a pandit trained in Bengal's tradition would know. Similarly, a South Indian Griha Pravesh follows a completely different Agamic tradition. PanditJi lets you filter by native region so you can find a pandit who truly understands your family's traditions.
